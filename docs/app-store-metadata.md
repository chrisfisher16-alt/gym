## App Store Description (4000 char limit)

FormIQ is the intelligent workout tracker that helps you build strength, track nutrition, and see real progress -- all in one clean, fast app.

**Smart Workout Logging**
Log your workouts with speed and precision. FormIQ tracks every set, rep, and weight, then automatically detects personal records as you lift. Progressive overload suggestions help you know exactly what to lift next based on your history.

- Quick set logging with weight and rep auto-fill
- Rest timer with customizable durations
- Warm-up and cool-down guidance
- Exercise library with 100+ movements
- Custom exercise creation
- Timed exercise support (planks, holds, carries)

**AI-Powered Coaching**
Get personalized coaching powered by AI that actually understands your training. Ask questions, get form advice, and receive workout suggestions based on your goals and history.

- Conversational AI coach with adjustable tone
- Workout generation based on your equipment and goals
- AI meal logging from photos or text descriptions
- Recipe suggestions matched to your macros

**Nutrition Tracking Made Simple**
Log meals in seconds with AI-powered food recognition. Track macros, hydration, and supplements without the tedious manual entry.

- AI photo meal logging -- snap and track
- Text-based quick logging
- Macro and calorie tracking
- Water intake monitoring
- Supplement reminders
- Grocery list generation from saved meals

**Progress You Can See**
Charts, PRs, and streaks that keep you motivated. FormIQ tracks your body measurements, strength progression, and consistency streaks so you can see how far you've come.

- Personal record tracking with PR celebrations
- Body measurement history
- Strength progression charts
- Workout streak tracking
- Achievement badges

**Health Integration**
Sync with Apple Health to keep all your fitness data in one place. Workouts logged in FormIQ automatically appear in Apple Health, and body weight syncs both ways.

**Built for Athletes**
FormIQ is designed by lifters, for lifters. The interface is fast, focused, and distraction-free. Dark and light modes look great in any gym lighting.

- Offline support -- log workouts without internet
- Dark and light mode
- Works on iPhone and iPad
- Data export in JSON or CSV

**Privacy First**
Your health data belongs to you. FormIQ never sells your data, never uses it for ads, and gives you full control to export or delete everything at any time.

Download FormIQ and start training smarter today.

## App Store Required Declarations

### Health Data Usage
- **Health data types accessed**: Workouts (read/write), Body Weight (read/write), Steps (read), Active Energy (read), Sleep (read)
- **Purpose**: "Used to track and display the user's fitness progress and sync workout data between FormIQ and Apple Health."
- **Not used for**: Advertising, data mining, or sharing with third parties

### Privacy Nutrition Labels (App Store Connect)

**Data Linked to You:**
- Contact Info: Email address (for account)
- Health & Fitness: Workout data, nutrition data, body measurements
- Identifiers: User ID

**Data Not Linked to You:**
- Usage Data: Anonymous analytics

**Data Not Collected:**
- Financial Info
- Location
- Contacts
- Search History
- Browsing History
- Sensitive Info
